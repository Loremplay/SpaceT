SpaceT

SpaceT is a decentralized open source protocol that will allow easy access to deFi, participation, agriculture, government voting, loans, and exchange services from a simple panel.

STAKING

Other yield farming protocols have provided huge investment returns for whales, but leave little for the average investor. SpaceT is the first performance agriculture platform that aims to curb inequality in performance agriculture.

YIELD FARMING

Users on our platform will have the opportunity to put their crypto assets to work those assets. Our yield farming program will be launching in the fourth quarter of 2022.

LENDING

Our lending protocol aims to simplify the process of lending and borrowing crypto assets while maintaining decentralization and transparency by leveraging superior capacity (i.e. high performance and smart contract functionality).

BORROW

Users are able to use their crypto assets as collateral to obtain a loan.

GOVERNANCE

SPC members can participate in the governance of the network to maintain an open and fair ecosystem at SpaceT. By holding SPC tokens, you are eligible to vote during the decision-making process. SPC users will collectively act as protocol governors.

SPACETSWAP

The SpaceT platform will feature a decentralized exchange where users can easily swap token pairs at a reduced fee.
